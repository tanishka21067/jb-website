Types of Rubber Products & Materials

At JB Enterprises, we offer a diverse range of rubber products crafted with precision and supported by our in-house die design and manufacturing capabilities.

Rubber Moulded Parts

Custom-shaped rubber components made using precision molds.

  • Used in automotive
  • Used in industrial, electrical, and sealing applications for durability and fit

Gaskets

Flat rubber components that create a seal between two surfaces to prevent leaks.

  • Used in engines
  • Used in pipelines, machinery, and electrical enclosures

O-Rings

Circular rubber rings that provide airtight and fluid-tight sealing.

  • Used in hydraulic systems
  • Used in pumps, valves, and engines

Seals

Flexible rubber barriers that prevent the entry or escape of fluids or gases.

  • Used in rotating shafts
  • Used in pipe joints and mechanical equipment

Bushings

Rubber sleeves that reduce vibration and wear between mechanical parts.

  • Used in suspension systems
  • Used in machinery joints and rotating parts

Grommets

Small rubber rings inserted into holes to protect wires or pipes from abrasion.

  • Used in electrical panels
  • Used in wiring systems and sheet metal parts

Rubber Washers

Flat rubber discs that distribute load and prevent leakage under fasteners.

  • Used in plumbing
  • Used in automotive and mechanical assemblies

Bellows / Boots

Flexible covers that protect moving parts from dust, dirt, and moisture.

  • Used in steering systems
  • Used in suspension joints and machinery

Rubber Plugs / Stoppers

Tapered rubber pieces used to seal or close off holes or tubes.

  • Used in laboratory glassware
  • Used in industrial piping and containers



Our in-house die design and manufacturing capabilities allow us to produce all types of dies, ensuring high precision and customization for every product.

Metal-Rubber Bonded Parts

Components where rubber is chemically bonded to metal for enhanced strength and flexibility.

  • Used in engine mounts
  • Used in anti-vibration systems, suspension parts, and noise-reducing devices

Anti-Vibration Mounts

Rubber elements designed to isolate vibrations and absorb shocks in mechanical systems.

  • Used in motors
  • Used in compressors, HVAC units, and industrial machinery

Rubber Pads / Sheets

Flat rubber materials available in various thicknesses and hardness levels.

  • Used for shock absorption
  • Used in flooring, gaskets, noise dampening, and protective linings

Rubber Rollers

Cylindrical rollers covered in rubber for controlled friction and movement.

  • Used in printing
  • Used in textile machines, packaging equipment, and conveyor systems

Rubber Diaphragms

Flexible rubber seals that respond to pressure differences or movement.

  • Used in pumps
  • Used in valves, regulators, and fuel or air control systems

Rubber Dampers

Rubber-based shock-absorbing devices used to control motion or vibration.

  • Used in mechanical systems
  • Used in vehicle parts and industrial equipment

Engine Mounts

Rubber-metal parts that absorb engine vibrations and secure it in place.

  • Used in vehicles
  • Used in generators and industrial engines for smooth operation

Rubber Couplings

Flexible rubber connectors that join two shafts to transmit torque.

  • Used in power transmission systems
  • Used in industrial motors and pumps

Oil Seals

Designed for sealing in automotive engines and systems.

  • High temperature resistance
  • Ensures leak-proof performance

Rubber Hoses

Flexible tubes made of durable rubber for transporting fluids or air.

  • Used in fuel systems, radiators
  • Used in fuel coolant lines, and vacuum systems.

Suspension Bushes

Vibration-damping rubber parts that reduce noise and harshness in vehicles.

  • Installed in control arms, stabilizer bars, and shock absorber mounts.

Shock Absorber Mounts

Rubber-based mounts that cushion vibrations and impacts from the road.

  • Used to attach shock absorbers to the vehicle frame or suspension.

Fuel System Seals

Rubber-based seals that prevent leaks in fuel systems.

  • Used in fuel pumps, injectors, tank openings, and pipe joints.

Dust Covers

Protective rubber components that shield parts from dirt, dust, and moisture.

  • Commonly used in suspension systems.
  • Commonly used in ball joints, and CV joints.

Door & Window Seals

Rubber profiles that provide insulation and prevent dust, noise, and water entry.

  • Used around car doors, trunks, windshields, and windows.

Silicone Rubber Components

Highly flexible and heat-resistant rubber ideal for sensitive environments.

  • Used in medical devices
  • Used in food-grade equipment, electronics, and automotive seals

EPDM Rubber Profiles

UV and weather-resistant rubber suitable for outdoor and sealing applications.

  • Used in automotive weather strips
  • Used in doors, windows, and roofing systems

Nitrile Rubber Parts

Oil- and fuel-resistant rubber with high durability.

  • Used in fuel hoses
  • Used in oil seals, gaskets, and industrial machinery

Viton Seals and Gaskets

Premium rubber known for superior chemical and temperature resistance.

  • Ideal for aerospace
  • Ideal for automotive fuel systems and chemical plants

High-Temperature Rubber Items

Engineered to withstand extreme heat without degradation.

  • Used in engines
  • Used in furnaces, industrial ovens, and insulation applications

Chemical-Resistant Rubber Products

Designed to resist harsh acids, alkalis, and solvents.

  • Used in pharmaceutical plants
  • Used in labs and chemical manufacturing

Rubber Expansion Joints

Flexible connectors that absorb vibration and thermal movement in piping systems.

  • Used in bridges
  • Used in water pipelines, HVAC ducts, and industrial structures

Bridge Bearing Pads

Support structures that allow controlled movement under load and temperature shifts.

  • Used beneath bridge decks
  • Used in buildings and heavy infrastructure foundations

Pipe Seals

Ensure leak-proof joints in piping systems.

  • Used in plumbing
  • Used in sewage systems and underground water lines

Manhole Gaskets

Rubber seals that prevent water ingress and odor escape in manholes.

  • Used in municipal drainage systems
  • Used in utility covers and waterworks
