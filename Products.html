Types of Rubber Products & Materials

At JB Enterprises, we offer a diverse range of rubber products crafted with precision and supported by our in-house die design and manufacturing capabilities.

Gaskets and Seals

Precision-engineered components designed to provide leak-proof performance across various industries.

  • Customizable for specific applications
  • Ensures reliability and durability

O-Rings

Versatile sealing rings available in multiple sizes for industrial and automotive use.

  • High flexibility and sealing efficiency
  • Suitable for diverse pressure conditions

Rubber Moulded Components

Custom-designed parts tailored for specialized machinery and unique operational needs.

  • Precision manufacturing for accuracy
  • Enhances machinery performance

Rubber Sheets

Versatile sheets offering insulation and protection for a wide range of applications.

  • Easy to cut and install
  • Provides robust protective layers

Vibration Dampers

Specialized components designed to reduce noise and vibration in heavy equipment.

  • Improves equipment lifespan
  • Enhances operational comfort



Our in-house die design and manufacturing capabilities allow us to produce all types of dies, ensuring high precision and customization for every product.

Nitrile (NBR)

A synthetic rubber with excellent resistance to oils and fuels, perfect for automotive use.

  • High oil resistance
  • Ideal for fuel system components
Image

PU Rubber

Known for high durability and abrasion resistance, suitable for demanding environments.

  • Long-lasting performance
  • Resistant to wear and tear

Neoprene (CR)

Offers balanced properties with weather resistance for general-purpose applications.

  • Good flexibility
  • Suitable for outdoor use

Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM)

Provides superior weathering and chemical resistance, ideal for outdoor conditions.

  • Excellent UV resistance
  • Resistant to acids and alkalis

Silicone (Q)

Offers high-temperature resistance, making it ideal for extreme conditions.

  • Stable at high temperatures
  • Flexible in cold environments

Viton (FKM)

Features exceptional chemical and heat resistance for specialized industrial applications.

  • Resists aggressive chemicals
  • Performs under high heat
