About Us

JB Enterprises is a trusted name in the rubber manufacturing industry, offering high-quality rubber and rubber component solutions since 2018. With over 7 years in business and a team of experts bringing more than 40 years of combined experience, we deliver unmatched expertise, precision, and reliability. We specialize in customized rubber products — including rubber moulded parts, gaskets, O-rings, and metal-rubber bonded parts — designed to meet the specific needs of diverse industries.

At JB Enterprises, we pride ourselves on:

  • Tailor-made rubber solutions.
  • Top-grade materials.
  • Timely deliveries.
  • And a customer-first approach that builds lasting partnerships

From concept to completion, we ensure every product meets your exact standards — on time, every time.
